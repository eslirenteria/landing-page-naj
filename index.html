ABOUT

The Mexican cuisine is the set of dishes and techniques culinary Mexico that are part of the traditions and common life of its inhabitants, enriched by the contributions of the various regions of the country, which derives from the experience of the prehispanic Mexico with the kitchen European, among other. The 16 of November of 2010, Mexican cuisine was recognized as Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity by Unesco.

Mexican cuisine has been influenced and has in turn influenced cuisines of other cultures, such as Spanish, French, African, Middle Eastern and Asian. It is testimony to the country's historical culture: many dishes originated in pre-Hispanic Mexico and other important moments in its history. There is a wide range of flavors, colors, textures and influences that make it a great attraction for nationals and foreigners: Mexico is famous for its cuisine.
